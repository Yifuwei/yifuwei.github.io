Personal Statement

I am a doctoral researcher specializing in Operational Research, with a core focus on cutting and packing optimization algorithms. My research involves developing cutting-edge methodologies for robotic disassembly and packing of irregular-shaped tokamak waste components, supported by a full studentship from UKAEA and UKRI.

With over 5 years of Python experience, I focus on GPU-accelerated (CUDA) 3D bin packing and high-performance computing (HPC) solutions for complex engineering problems.
