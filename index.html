Personal Statement

I am a doctoral researcher in Operational Research and Optimisation, specialising in large-scale modelling, simulation, and optimisation of complex engineering systems under real-world constraints. I develop high-performance algorithms, simulation frameworks, and decision-support tools to improve system efficiency, optimise overall cost, and analyse performance across scenarios.

My current PhD project focuses on optimal cutting, grasping and packing of irregular-shaped tokamak waste components, aligned with digital-twin style decision support for large-scale decommissioning.

Interests: Combinatorial optimisation · Cutting and packing · Digital twin modelling
